The Shore Acres Club House

Overview

The Shore Acres Clubhouse is one of the most important assets to our community. The facility plays host to most of our board and general meetings as well as many gatherings, social and community improvement events. In addition to being a great event space complete with a bar, two seperate bathrooms, a seperate food prep space, and 2000+ square feet of open floor, the clubhouse features plenty of parking, a playground, basketball court, and open greenspace. The clubhouse also serves as an important fundraiser for the community and is available for private rentals to imporvement association members, nonmembers, and essentially anyone looking for event space.
